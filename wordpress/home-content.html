Powder coating · Sheffield

A hard-wearing finish, applied properly the first time

We are a Sheffield powder coating specialist working with everyone from fabricators and fleet operators to homeowners restoring a single set of alloys. Every job is blasted, pre-treated and coated in-house, so nothing leaves the unit until it is right.

Powder coating gives a thicker, tougher and more even finish than wet paint — resistant to chips, corrosion and UV — in virtually any RAL colour and a range of gloss levels and textures. No job too big, no job too small.
